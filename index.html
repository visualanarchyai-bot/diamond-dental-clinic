About Diamond Dental Clinic

Diamond Dental Clinic has been serving patients in Imphal since 2016, providing quality dental care in a comfortable environment. Our clinic focuses on modern dental treatments, patient comfort, and restoring beautiful smiles with professional care.

Led by Dr Bushini, our experienced team is dedicated to delivering gentle, effective dental treatments using the latest technology. We believe everyone deserves a healthy, confident smile.
